About

Ben Greener - Vocals/Guitar

Ben is the main songwriter for The Smoking Rolo Sideshow, co-writing the first album with Greig Waugh and the second album by himself. He cites his influences as Mark Lanegan, Elvis Costello, and Death From Above.

Kevin McEwan - Lead Guitar/Vocals

Kev began playing live shows with his first band "Placid Blue" at the age of fifteen, and hasn't stopped playing since. His influences include Sonic Youth, Butthole Surfers, and Nirvana, bringing a raw and unpredictable edge to the sound of the band.

Greig Waugh - Rhythm Guitar

Greig is an avid guitar builder, and enjoys experimenting with the potential of analogue and digital music. His influences include Kyuss, Primus, Grinderman, and Stephen Brodsky's Octave Museum.

Jonathan Frith - Bass Guitar

Jonathan switched from lead guitar to bass as a teenager, and his playing style helps drive the band's complex sound. His influences include Foo Fighters, Queens of the Stone Age, and Band of Horses.

Michael Archbold - Keyboards

Michael began playing drums in high school while dabbling with the keyboards, before making the switch full-time with a variety of funk and soul bands around Nottingham. His influences include Money Mark, Ray Manzarek, andBooker T. Jones.

Michael Ashurst - Drums

"Mick the Stick" is an Amble lad, and longtime collaborator with Kevin McEwan (being the drummer for Placid Blue in the early 2000s). Michael's influences include Zebrahead, Less than Jake, and Reel Big Fish.
